About me

Hi there! My name is Akshay Dilip Dhivare , and I'm a Front-End Web Developer based in Mumbai. I'm passionate about creating beautiful and user-friendly websites that make a difference in people's lives. and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet.

I earned my Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from B. R. Harne College Of Engineering College in Karav, Maharashtra in 2022. After completing my education, I started my career as a Front-End Web Developer, working on various projects for clients in different industries. Currently, I work at IT Company as a Front-End Web Developer, where I help build and maintain websites for clients.

I specialize in Front-End Web Development, with expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap. Currently , I'm exploring React JS , Material UI and related technologies. I'm always looking for ways to improve my skills and stay up-to-date with the latest trends in the field.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me. I'm always excited to meet new people and collaborate on new projects, so feel free to reach out if you have any questions or ideas you'd like to discuss!
